================================================================
MONTHLY GRAPE SALES REPORT - ARAB MARKETS
================================================================
Reporting Period: Q1 2026 (January - March)
Markets Covered: UAE, Saudi Arabia, Egypt, Jordan, Lebanon

================================================================
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================
Total grape sales across all Arab markets reached $12.4M in Q1 2026,
showing a +15.3% growth compared to Q4 2025. The highest demand was
observed for Red Globe and Thompson Seedless varieties, particularly
during Ramadan preparations.

KEY INSIGHTS
================================================================
- Ramadan Effect: March sales increased by 28% due to Ramadan 2026
- Premium Segment: Black Magic grapes saw 45% volume growth in UAE
- Export Trend: Increased demand for seedless varieties (+32% YoY)
- Seasonal Dip: Lower sales in Egypt due to domestic harvest season
